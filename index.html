Hi! I'm Will.

I am a computer science and mathematics student at the University of Wisconsin, a self-taught programmer, a creative thinker, an innovator, and a positive self-starter.

Before college I was born and raised in Wauwatosa, Wisconsin. Through my youth I was instilled with the confidence to travel and the curiousity to learn. Currently I intern @Amway at their World Headquarters in Grand Rapids, Michigan as a DevOps Engineer.

I began to fully immerse myself in code when I recognized my interest in technology just a short time ago. I made the decison to switch from an Industrial Engineering track to a Computer Science track coupled with Mathematics to fulfill my new passion and pursuit. Since then, I have loved software engineering and the challenges it brings. I plan to contribute greatly to new projects and deployments and eventually transition to a leadership/management role.

Other interests of mine include working out, cooking, building cool projects, playing chess and traveling wherever and whenever I can. As the great Anthony Bourdain once said, "Travel is about the gorgeous feeling of teetering in the unknown." In my opinion, life is filled with those feelings. The more I don't know, the more I want to know, and so I strive to make the unknown, known.
